Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar
Arrival & Transfer: Upon arrival at Srinagar Airport, meet our representative and transfer to your hotel.
Mughal Gardens: Visit the exquisite Mughal Gardens - Chashma Shahi, Shalimar Bagh, and Nishat Bagh, showcasing stunning landscapes and Mughal architecture.
Shankaracharya Temple: Visit the ancient Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic views of Srinagar and Dal Lake.
Handicrafts Showroom: Explore authentic Kashmiri handicrafts including silk carpets and pashmina shawls at a local showroom.
Shikara Ride & Tulip Garden: Enjoy a 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake. Seasonal visit to Tulip Garden (March-May).
Evening Stroll: Leisurely walk along Boulevard Road by Dal Lake before returning to your hotel for overnight stay.
Day 2: Sonmarg Day Trip
Breakfast & Transfer: After breakfast, drive to Sonmarg "Meadow of Gold" (approx. 84 km, 2.5-3 hours).
Sonmarg Exploration: Situated at 9,000 ft in the Sindh Valley, Sonmarg is surrounded by majestic mountains and vibrant meadows.
Thajiwas Glacier: Optional pony ride to Thajiwas Glacier or visit Zero Point by union cab (direct payment).
Trekking Base: Sonmarg serves as base for treks to high-altitude Himalayan lakes.
Return to Hotel: Return to your Srinagar hotel for overnight stay.
Day 3: Gulmarg Day Trip
Breakfast & Transfer: After breakfast, drive to Gulmarg (approx. 51 km, 1-1.5 hours).
Gulmarg Exploration: Gulmarg, at 2,730 meters, is renowned for its ski slopes and one of the world's highest golf courses.
Scenic Views: Enjoy breathtaking views from Tangmarg to Gulmarg. On clear days, glimpse the majestic Nanga Parbat peak.
Adventure Options: Trek to Khilanmarg (3-4 hours) or take the Gondola cable car to Kongdori for spectacular views (tickets not included).
Return to Hotel: Return to your Srinagar hotel for overnight stay.
Day 4: Pahalgam Day Trip
Breakfast & Transfer: After breakfast, drive to Pahalgam "Valley of Shepherds" (approx. 90 km, 2.5 hours).
Nature Immersion: Admire Pahalgam's serene beauty surrounded by pine forests and the Lidder River.
Optional Pony Ride: Enjoy a pony ride to nearby attractions like Chandanwadi (direct payment to operator).
Riverside Walk: Peaceful walk along the Lidder River for nature enthusiasts.
Trekking Base: Pahalgam serves as base for several trekking routes and the Amarnath Pilgrimage.
Return & Houseboat Stay: Return to Srinagar for overnight stay in a traditional houseboat on Dal Lake.
Day 5: Departure from Srinagar
Breakfast & Check-out: After breakfast, check out from your houseboat (standard check-out: 12:00 PM).
Airport Transfer: Transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey with beautiful Kashmir memories.
