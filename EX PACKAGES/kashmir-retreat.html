Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival Day Srinagar
Arrival & Transfer: Upon arrival at Srinagar Airport, meet our representative and transfer to your hotel.
Mughal Gardens: Visit the Mughal Gardens - Chashma Shahi, Shalimar Bagh, and Nishat Bagh - showcasing stunning views and Mughal architecture.
Shankaracharya Temple: Visit Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic city views and Dal Lake vistas.
Handicrafts Showroom: Stop at a local handicrafts showroom to explore Kashmiri silk carpets and shawls.
Shikara Ride & Tulip Garden: Enjoy a 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake and explore Tulip Garden (seasonal: March–May).
Evening Stroll: Enjoy a leisurely stroll along Boulevard Road by Dal Lake before returning to your hotel.
Day 2: Sonmarg Excursion
Breakfast & Transfer: After breakfast, drive to Sonmarg (84 km, approx. 2.5 hours), "The Meadow of Gold."
Scenic Sonmarg: Enjoy breathtaking views of the Sindh Valley and Himalayan peaks. Sonmarg serves as the base for treks to high-altitude lakes.
Optional Activities: Take a pony ride to Thajiwas Glacier or visit Zero Point by union cab (additional cost).
Return to Hotel: Return to your hotel in Srinagar for overnight stay.
Day 3: Gulmarg Excursion
Breakfast & Transfer: After breakfast, drive to Gulmarg (51 km, approx. 1.5 hours).
Gulmarg Highlights: Explore this world-class destination known for its ski slopes and the highest 18-hole golf course.
Scenic Views: Admire the beautiful Tangmarg Valley en route. If weather permits, catch a glimpse of Nanga Parbat.
Optional Activities: Trek to Khilanmarg (3-4 hours) or take the Gondola cable car to Kongdori for breathtaking views (additional cost).
Return to Hotel: Return to your hotel in Srinagar for overnight stay.
Day 4: Srinagar to Pahalgam
Breakfast & Transfer: After breakfast, transfer to Pahalgam, the "Valley of Shepherds."
Nature Exploration: Enjoy the serene landscapes and admire the beauty of this tranquil destination along the Lidder River.
Optional Activities: Enjoy a pony ride to Chandanwadi or take a peaceful nature walk along the river (additional cost).
Overnight Stay: Overnight stay in Pahalgam Hotel.
Day 5: Pahalgam to Srinagar
Breakfast & Sightseeing: After breakfast, enjoy sightseeing in Pahalgam.
Optional Activities: Take a pony ride to Baisaran Valley (additional cost).
Trekking & Amarnath Base: Visit the Amarnath Yatra base camp and explore nearby trekking routes.
Return to Srinagar: Drive back to Srinagar for overnight stay in a traditional houseboat.
Day 6: Departure
Breakfast & Airport Transfer: After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey.
