4 Nights in Srinagar + 1 Night in Pahalgam + 1 Night in Houseboat (MAPAI Plan: Daily Breakfast + Dinner). Experience the essence of paradise with serene lakes, meadows, and mountains in Kashmir's most complete itinerary.

Dal Lake, Srinagar

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar
Arrival & Transfer
Meet our representative at Srinagar Airport and transfer to your hotel.
Mughal Gardens
Visit Mughal Gardens (Chashma Shahi, Nishat Bagh, and Shalimar Bagh) — beautiful examples of Persian-influenced design and panoramic views.
Shankaracharya Temple
Visit Shankaracharya Temple overlooking Dal Lake.
Handicrafts Showroom
Stop by a handicraft showroom for authentic Kashmiri carpets and shawls.
Shikara Ride & Tulip Garden
Enjoy a 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake and visit the Tulip Garden (March–May).
Evening Stroll
Evening stroll by Boulevard Road. Overnight stay in Srinagar.
Day 2: Sonmarg Excursion
Breakfast & Transfer
After breakfast, drive to Sonmarg ("Meadow of Gold"), 84 km from Srinagar at an altitude of 9,000 ft.
Scenic Exploration
Explore the Sindh Valley's alpine meadows and snow peaks.
Optional Activities
Optional pony ride to Thajiwas Glacier or union cab ride to Zero Point.
Return to Hotel
Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.
Day 3: Gulmarg Excursion
Breakfast & Transfer
Breakfast and drive to Gulmarg (51 km / 1.5 hrs).
Scenic Views
En route, enjoy scenic Tangmarg Valley.
Gulmarg Highlights
Visit the world's highest 18-hole golf course and ski slopes.
Optional Activities
Optional Gondola ride to Kongdori or trek to Khilanmarg for Himalayan views.
Return to Hotel
Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.
Day 4: Doodhpatri Excursion
Breakfast & Transfer
Breakfast and drive to Doodhpatri (40 km, Budgam district).
Nature Exploration
Explore meadows, pine forests, and river streams.
Return to Hotel
Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.
Day 5: Srinagar to Pahalgam
Breakfast & Transfer
Breakfast and drive to Pahalgam, the "Valley of Shepherds."
Scenic Beauty
Enjoy the scenic Lidder River and lush greenery.
Optional Activities
Optional pony ride to Chandanwadi, Aru Valley, or Betaab Valley (direct payment).
Overnight Stay
Overnight stay in Pahalgam Hotel.
Day 6: Pahalgam to Srinagar (Houseboat Stay)
Breakfast & Sightseeing
Breakfast and local sightseeing in Pahalgam.
Optional Activities
Optional pony ride to Baisaran Valley.
Return to Srinagar
Return drive to Srinagar and check into your houseboat.
Houseboat Stay
Overnight stay on Dal Lake.
Day 7: Departure
Breakfast & Airport Transfer
Breakfast and transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey.
