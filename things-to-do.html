Things to Do in Kashmir

Unforgettable experiences await you

Best Time for Activities

Kashmir offers different activities throughout the year. Plan your visit based on your interests!

Winter

Dec - Feb
Skiing, Snowboarding

Spring

Mar - May
Gardens, Trekking

Summer

Jun - Aug
Rafting, Camping

Autumn

Sep - Nov
Photography, Hiking

Shikara Ride

Relaxation

Experience the serene beauty of Dal Lake on a traditional wooden shikara. Glide through floating gardens and witness the magical sunrise or sunset over the mountains.

1-2 hours ₹500-800 4.8/5

Skiing & Snowboarding

Adventure

Hit the slopes at Gulmarg, one of Asia's premier ski destinations. Professional instructors and world-class equipment available for all skill levels.

Full Day ₹3000-5000 4.9/5

Mountain Trekking

Adventure

Explore Kashmir's pristine valleys and mountain passes. Trek through alpine meadows, glaciers, and remote villages with experienced guides.

3-7 days ₹5000-15000 4.7/5

River Rafting

Adventure

Navigate the rapids of Lidder River in Pahalgam. Experience thrilling white-water rafting with safety equipment and expert guides.

2-3 hours ₹1500-2500 4.6/5

Gulmarg Gondola

Nature

Ride Asia's highest cable car to 13,000 feet. Witness breathtaking panoramic views of snow-capped Himalayan peaks and valleys.

2-3 hours ₹1500-2000 5.0/5

Horse Riding

Nature

Explore scenic valleys on horseback. Ride through meadows, along rivers, and up mountain trails with local guides.

1-4 hours ₹800-2000 4.5/5

Mughal Gardens Tour

Cultural

Visit historic Mughal gardens including Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. Experience royal landscaping and architecture.

Half Day ₹500-1000 4.8/5

Photography Tours

Cultural

Capture Kashmir's stunning landscapes with professional photography guides. Learn techniques while exploring the most photogenic spots.

Full Day ₹2000-4000 4.9/5

Camping & Bonfire

Adventure

Sleep under the stars in pristine valleys. Enjoy bonfire nights, local music, traditional food, and unparalleled natural beauty.

Overnight ₹2000-3500 4.7/5

Local Markets & Crafts

Cultural

Explore traditional markets and handicraft shops. Buy authentic Pashmina shawls, carpets, paper mache, and Kashmiri spices.

2-4 hours Varies 4.6/5

Houseboat Experience

Relaxation

Stay in a luxury houseboat on Dal Lake. Experience traditional Kashmiri hospitality with modern amenities and stunning lake views.

Overnight ₹3000-8000 4.9/5

Kashmiri Food Tour

Cultural

Savor authentic Wazwan cuisine and local delicacies. Visit traditional restaurants and street food spots with culinary experts.

3-4 hours ₹1500-2500 4.8/5
