Detailed Itinerary — Admirable Kashmir (4N–5D)

Day 1: Arrival Day – Srinagar
Airport Pickup
Upon arrival at Srinagar Airport, meet our representative and transfer to your hotel.
Mughal Gardens
Visit Chashma Shahi, Shalimar Bagh, and Nishat Bagh.
Shankaracharya Temple
Panoramic city views from the temple.
Handicraft Showroom
Explore Kashmiri carpets and shawls.
Shikara Ride
Enjoy a 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
Dinner & Overnight
Return to hotel for dinner and overnight stay.
Day 2: Sonmarg Day Trip
Drive to Sonmarg
After breakfast, drive to Sonmarg (“Meadow of Gold”), 84 km from Srinagar.
Thajiwas Glacier
Visit Thajiwas Glacier (pony ride optional) and enjoy snow-capped mountain views.
Zero Point
Explore Zero Point if time permits.
Dinner & Overnight
Return to Srinagar hotel for dinner and overnight stay.
Day 3: Gulmarg Day Trip
Drive to Gulmarg
After breakfast, head toward Gulmarg (51 km, 1–1.5 hrs). Enjoy views from Tangmarg to Gulmarg.
Gondola Ride / Trek
Optional Gondola cable car ride to Kongdori or trek to Khilanmarg (3–4 hrs).
Dinner & Overnight
Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.
Day 4: Pahalgam Day Trip
Transfer to Pahalgam
After breakfast, transfer to Pahalgam (“Valley of Shepherds”).
Pony Rides / River Walks
Enjoy scenic drives, optional pony rides to Chandanwadi, or peaceful river walks.
Amarnath Pilgrimage
Pahalgam also serves as the base for the Amarnath pilgrimage.
Houseboat Stay
Return to Srinagar and check into the houseboat for overnight stay.
Day 5: Departure
Departure
After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey.
