Dal Lake, Srinagar

Discover the Magic of Kashmir

Our 5-day Kashmir Tour Package takes you on a comprehensive journey through the stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Kashmir. From the serene Dal Lake to the breathtaking valleys of Gulmarg and Sonmarg, experience the best that Kashmir has to offer.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Airport Pickup

Warm welcome at Srinagar International Airport by our representative who will assist with your transfer to the hotel/houseboat.

Check-in

Check into your hotel or premium houseboat on Dal Lake, depending on your booking preference.

Evening Shikara Ride

Enjoy a relaxing Shikara ride on the famous Dal Lake, witnessing the floating gardens and local life on the lake.

Dinner

Enjoy a delicious welcome dinner with authentic Kashmiri flavors at your accommodation.

Day 2: Srinagar City Tour

Breakfast

Start your day with a hearty breakfast at your accommodation.

Mughal Gardens Tour

Visit the famous Mughal Gardens including Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), Shalimar Bagh (Garden of Love), and Chashme Shahi (Royal Spring), featuring terraced lawns, cascading fountains, and flowerbeds.

Historical Sites

Visit the sacred Hazratbal Shrine, known for its pristine white marble architecture, and the ancient Shankaracharya Temple offering panoramic views of the city.

Local Market Visit

Explore the local markets for traditional Kashmiri handicrafts including pashmina shawls, wood carvings, and carpets.

Return to Accommodation

Return to your accommodation for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Gulmarg Excursion

Early Breakfast

Start your day with an early breakfast at your accommodation.

Drive to Gulmarg

Embark on a scenic 2-hour drive to Gulmarg, also known as the 'Meadow of Flowers', situated at an altitude of 8,700 ft.

Gondola Ride

Experience the world's second highest operating cable car, offering stunning views of snow-capped mountains (optional, tickets not included).

Leisure Activities

Enjoy leisure activities such as golf, pony riding, or simply taking in the beautiful mountain views.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Sonmarg Excursion

Early Breakfast

Enjoy an early breakfast before your day trip.

Drive to Sonmarg

Embark on a scenic 3-hour drive to Sonmarg, also known as the 'Meadow of Gold', located at an altitude of 9,200 ft.

Thajiwas Glacier

Visit the magnificent Thajiwas Glacier (horseback ride optional, fee not included), surrounded by snow-capped mountains.

Photography

Capture the breathtaking landscapes of meadows, lakes, and glaciers.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5: Departure

Breakfast

Enjoy your final breakfast at your accommodation.

Last-Minute Shopping

Time permitting, do some last-minute shopping for souvenirs.

Check-out

Complete the check-out formalities from your accommodation.

Airport Transfer

Transfer to Srinagar International Airport for your onward journey with cherished memories of Kashmir.
