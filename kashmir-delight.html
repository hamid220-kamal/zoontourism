Experience the Paradise on Earth

Our 4-day Kashmir Delight package offers the perfect introduction to the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir, known as "Paradise on Earth." Enjoy comfortable stays, iconic Shikara rides, and scenic day trips to enchanting destinations like Gulmarg and Pahalgam.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Airport Pickup

Our representative will receive you at Srinagar International Airport and transfer you to your houseboat.

Houseboat Check-in

Experience the traditional Kashmiri hospitality as you check into your luxurious houseboat on Dal Lake.

Evening Shikara Ride

Enjoy a relaxing 1-hour Shikara ride on the picturesque Dal Lake, witnessing the floating gardens and local way of life.

Welcome Dinner

Savor a delicious welcome dinner at the houseboat with authentic Kashmiri cuisine.

Day 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Breakfast

Enjoy a hearty breakfast at the houseboat.

Mughal Gardens Tour

Visit the famous Mughal Gardens including Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi, known for their terraced lawns, fountains, and gorgeous flowerbeds.

Heritage Walk

Visit Shankaracharya Temple and Hazratbal Shrine to learn about the diverse cultural heritage of Kashmir.

Local Market Visit

Experience the vibrant markets of Srinagar, where you can shop for traditional Kashmiri handicrafts, pashmina shawls, and dry fruits.

Return to Houseboat

Return to your houseboat for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Day Trip to Gulmarg

Early Breakfast

Start your day with an early breakfast at the houseboat.

Drive to Gulmarg

Embark on a scenic 2-hour drive to Gulmarg, known as the 'Meadow of Flowers', situated at an altitude of 8,700 ft.

Gondola Ride

Take a thrilling gondola cable car ride to Apharwat Peak, offering panoramic views of the surrounding Himalayan ranges (optional, tickets not included).

Leisure Activities

Enjoy leisure time for horse riding or simply relaxing amidst the beautiful landscapes.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your houseboat in Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Departure

Breakfast

Enjoy your final breakfast at the houseboat.

Check-out

Complete the check-out formalities from your houseboat.

Airport Transfer

Transfer to Srinagar International Airport for your onward journey.
