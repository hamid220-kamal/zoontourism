Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar
Airport Pickup
Warm welcome at Srinagar International Airport by our representative who will assist with your transfer to the hotel/houseboat.
Check-in
Check into your hotel or premium houseboat on Dal Lake, depending on your booking preference.
Evening Shikara Ride
Enjoy a relaxing Shikara ride on the famous Dal Lake, witnessing the floating gardens and local life on the lake.
Dinner
Enjoy a delicious welcome dinner with authentic Kashmiri flavors at your accommodation.
Day 2: Gulmarg Day Trip
Scenic Drive
Drive from Srinagar to Gulmarg (approx 51 km). If weather permits, catch a glimpse of Nanga Parbat.
Adventure & Sightseeing
Trek to Khilanmarg (3–4 hours) or take the Gondola cable car to Kongdori (optional, tickets not included).
Return & Stay
Return to Srinagar hotel for overnight stay.
Day 3: Pahalgam Day Trip
Transfer to Pahalgam
Drive to Pahalgam (“Valley of Shepherds”).
Nature & Pony Rides
Enjoy peaceful walks along the river and optional pony rides to nearby spots like Chandanwadi (direct hire).
Trekking & Pilgrimage
Pahalgam is the base for Amarnath pilgrimage and offers trekking routes.
Houseboat Stay
Return to Srinagar and check in to houseboat for overnight stay.
Day 4: Departure
Departure
After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for onward journey.
