About Us

Welcome to ZoonTourism

Discover the Magic of Kashmir

Our Journey Began with a Vision: ZoonTourism was founded with a simple yet powerful belief—that Kashmir deserves to be experienced not as a tourist destination, but as a place of personal transformation and cultural connection. The name "Zoon," meaning moonlight in Kashmiri, symbolizes the gentle illumination we bring to every traveler's journey.

Unlike conventional tour operators, we don't just show you Kashmir—we invite you to live it. From sunrise over Dal Lake to midnight tea in local Wangun homes, from trekking through alpine meadows to learning ancient Kashmiri crafts, our packages are designed to create meaningful bonds between you and this enchanting land. We believe authentic travel happens when you step off the beaten path and embrace the local way of life.

Why Choose ZoonTourism? We employ over 200 local guides, homestay owners, and artisans, ensuring your tourism dollars directly benefit Kashmir's communities. Our carbon-neutral initiatives, support for local businesses, and commitment to preserving traditional arts make us Kashmir's most responsible travel partner. Every rupee spent with us helps sustain Kashmir's culture and environment for future generations.

Safety First

We maintain the highest safety standards. All guides are trained in first aid, all vehicles are regularly inspected, and we maintain partnerships with hospitals across Kashmir.

Authentic Experiences

Sleep in a Kashmiri houseboat, learn to make traditional Wazwan, visit artisan workshops, and celebrate with local families—not as observers, but as honored guests.

Sustainability

We've planted over 50,000 trees, support 15 women-led businesses, and use only eco-friendly practices to preserve Kashmir's pristine natural beauty.

Excellence

From 5-star houseboats to gourmet Kashmiri meals prepared by award-winning chefs, every detail is curated for unforgettable memories.

About Us

Make Your Tour Memorable and Safe With Us

The Zoon Difference: We are not just another travel agency. ZoonTourism is a community of passionate travelers and local enthusiasts dedicated to reimagining Kashmir tourism. Our founder, inspired by a transformative solo journey through Kashmir's valleys, created Zoon to bridge the gap between travelers seeking authenticity and local communities eager to share their heritage.

Our Commitment: Every package we design goes through a rigorous sustainability audit. We partner only with businesses that treat workers fairly, conserve the environment, and contribute to local prosperity. Our guides are not just trained professionals—they're storytellers, historians, and advocates for Kashmir's preservation. We've trained over 500 local youth in hospitality, creating economic opportunities in remote villages.

A Legacy of Impact: Since our inception, we've helped restore three ancient temples, supported 8 women's cooperatives in traditional embroidery, and donated educational scholarships to 45 underprivileged children in Kashmir. Travel with us, and you become part of this mission.
