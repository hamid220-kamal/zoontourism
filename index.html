Discovering Srinagar: A Valley of Wonders

January 11, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

The Summer Capital of Kashmir

Srinagar, the crown jewel of Kashmir, sits at 1,585 meters on the banks of the Jhelum River. Known as the "City of Lakes," it has been enchanting travelers for centuries with its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage.

Must-Visit Attractions
  • Dal Lake: Experience the iconic houseboats and colorful shikaras
  • Mughal Gardens: Explore Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi
  • Old City: Visit Jamia Masjid and discover traditional Kashmiri architecture
  • Shankaracharya Temple: Ancient temple offering panoramic valley views
Travel Tips

Best time to visit is April-October for pleasant weather, or December-February for snow experiences.

Adventure in Gulmarg: Sky High Thrills

January 15, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

Asia's Premier Ski Destination

At 2,650 meters, Gulmarg transforms into a winter wonderland from December to March. Home to the world's second-highest cable car (Gulmarg Gondola), it offers breathtaking views and thrilling adventures year-round.

Adventure Activities
  • Gulmarg Gondola: Ride to 3,979 meters for stunning Himalayan panoramas
  • Skiing & Snowboarding: World-class powder snow for enthusiasts
  • Golf Course: One of the highest golf courses in the world
  • Trekking: Summer trails to Khilanmarg and Alpather Lake
Travel Tips

Winter (Dec-Mar) for skiing, Summer (Apr-Jun) for meadows and trekking.

Plan Your Gulmarg Adventure
Pahalgam: Nature's Paradise Awaits You

January 22, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

The Valley of Shepherds

Pahalgam, at 2,740 meters, is where the Lidder River flows through pristine meadows surrounded by pine forests. This tranquil town serves as the base camp for the annual Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage.

Explore Pahalgam
  • Betaab Valley: Stunning landscapes and crystal-clear streams
  • Aru Valley: Starting point for Kolahoi Glacier and Tarsar Marsar treks
  • Lidder River: Perfect for trout fishing and gentle rafting
  • Baisaran (Mini Switzerland): Rolling meadows with panoramic views
Travel Tips

Best visited April-November. Pony rides are popular for exploring nearby valleys.
