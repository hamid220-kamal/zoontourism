Discovering Srinagar: A Valley of Wonders

January 11, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

The Summer Capital of Kashmir

Srinagar, the crown jewel of Kashmir, sits at 1,585 meters on the banks of the Jhelum River. Known as the "City of Lakes," it has been enchanting travelers for centuries with its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage.

Must-Visit Attractions
  • Dal Lake: Experience the iconic houseboats and colorful shikaras that define Srinagar's charm
  • Mughal Gardens: Explore Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi - masterpieces of Mughal landscaping
  • Old City: Walk through historic lanes, visit Jamia Masjid, and discover traditional Kashmiri architecture
  • Shankaracharya Temple: Ancient Hindu temple offering panoramic views of the valley
Travel Tips

Best time to visit is April-October for pleasant weather, or December-February for snow experiences. Plan at least 3-4 days to properly explore Srinagar and nearby attractions.

Adventure in Gulmarg: Sky High Thrills

January 15, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

Asia's Premier Ski Destination

At 2,650 meters, Gulmarg transforms into a winter wonderland from December to March. Home to the world's second-highest cable car (Gulmarg Gondola), it offers breathtaking views and thrilling adventures year-round.

Adventure Activities
  • Gulmarg Gondola: Ride to 3,979 meters at Kongdori and Apharwat Peak for stunning Himalayan panoramas
  • Skiing & Snowboarding: World-class powder snow attracts enthusiasts from around the globe
  • Golf Course: One of the highest golf courses in the world, set amidst alpine meadows
  • Trekking: Summer trails through wildflower meadows to Khilanmarg and Alpather Lake
Travel Tips

Winter (Dec-Mar) for skiing, Summer (Apr-Jun) for meadows and trekking. The Gondola can have long queues in peak season - arrive early!

Pahalgam: Nature's Paradise Awaits

January 22, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

The Valley of Shepherds

Pahalgam, at 2,740 meters, is where the Lidder River flows through pristine meadows surrounded by pine forests. This tranquil town serves as the base camp for the annual Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage.

Explore Pahalgam
  • Betaab Valley: Named after the Bollywood film, this valley features stunning landscapes and crystal-clear streams
  • Aru Valley: Starting point for treks to Kolahoi Glacier and Tarsar Marsar Lakes
  • Lidder River: Perfect for trout fishing and gentle rafting experiences
  • Baisaran (Mini Switzerland): Rolling meadows with panoramic mountain views
Travel Tips

Best visited April-November. Pony rides are popular for exploring nearby valleys. The Amarnath Yatra season (Jul-Aug) sees heavy crowds.

Sonamarg: The Golden Meadow of Kashmir

February 5, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

Gateway to Ladakh

At 2,800 meters, Sonamarg ("Meadow of Gold") is the last major Kashmiri town before the Zoji La pass leading to Ladakh. In summer, its meadows burst with golden flowers, giving the valley its poetic name.

Key Attractions
  • Thajiwas Glacier: A stunning accessible glacier just 3 km from town, reachable by pony or on foot
  • Zoji La Pass: Historic mountain pass on the ancient Silk Road at 3,528 meters
  • Krishansar & Vishansar Lakes: Twin alpine lakes accessible via scenic multi-day treks
  • Sindh River: Excellent trout fishing in crystal-clear waters
Travel Tips

Open May-November (road closes in winter due to heavy snowfall). Perfect for day trips from Srinagar (80 km) or as a overnight stop en route to Ladakh.

Dal Lake: Life on Kashmir's Floating Paradise

February 12, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

The Jewel in Kashmir's Crown

Spanning 18 square kilometers, Dal Lake is not just a tourist attractionâ€”it's a living, breathing community. Generations of families live on its waters, maintaining the unique culture that makes Kashmir so special.

Dal Lake Experiences
  • Houseboat Stay: Spend a night on a traditional wooden houseboat with handcrafted interiors
  • Shikara Rides: Glide across the lake in colorful wooden boats, especially magical at sunset
  • Floating Gardens: See unique "rad" gardens where vegetables grow on floating earth beds
  • Morning Markets: Wake early to witness the floating vegetable market where vendors trade from boats
Travel Tips

Negotiate shikara rates before boarding. Sunset and sunrise rides are most beautiful. Winter offers misty, ethereal views when the lake partially freezes.

Betaab Valley: Bollywood's Favorite Kashmir Paradise

February 18, 2025 | ZoonTourism Team

A Cinematic Paradise

Named after the 1983 Bollywood blockbuster "Betaab" starring Sunny Deol, this valley 15 km from Pahalgam has become one of Kashmir's most photographed locations. Its dramatic landscapes have featured in numerous films since.

Valley Highlights
  • Lidder Stream: The crystal-clear Lidder River flows through the valley, perfect for photography
  • Snow-capped Peaks: Dramatic mountain backdrop visible year-round
  • Lush Meadows: Emerald green pastures ideal for picnics and nature walks
  • Film Locations: Recognize spots from famous Bollywood movies shot here
Travel Tips

Best combined with Aru Valley as a day trip from Pahalgam. Entry fee applies. Morning visits offer the best lighting for photography. Ponies available for exploring further into the valley.
