Dal Lake, Srinagar

Complete Kashmir Experience

Our 6-day Kashmir Exploration Package offers a comprehensive journey through the jewel of India. Experience the breathtaking landscapes of Srinagar, Gulmarg, Sonmarg, and Pahalgam while immersing yourself in the rich cultural heritage of this region.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Airport Pickup

Our representative will greet you at Srinagar International Airport and assist with your transfer to the hotel/houseboat.

Check-in

Settle into your luxurious accommodation, either a premium houseboat on Dal Lake or a hotel, based on your preference.

Evening Shikara Ride

Experience the tranquility of Dal Lake with a traditional Shikara ride, passing through floating gardens and vibrant local markets.

Welcome Dinner

Enjoy a traditional Kashmiri Wazwan dinner at your accommodation with authentic local cuisine.

Day 2: Srinagar City Tour

Breakfast

Begin your day with a hearty Kashmiri breakfast at your accommodation.

Mughal Gardens Tour

Visit the famous Mughal Gardens of Srinagar including Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. These terraced gardens with fountains and flower beds showcase the distinctive Mughal landscape architecture.

Historical Monuments

Visit the Hazratbal Shrine, famous for housing a relic of Prophet Muhammad, and the ancient Shankaracharya Temple perched atop a hill offering panoramic views of the city and Dal Lake.

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Explore local artisans' workshops and markets to witness the making of traditional Kashmiri handicrafts like pashmina shawls, carpets, paper-mache, and woodwork.

Return to Accommodation

Return to your accommodation for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Gulmarg Excursion

Early Breakfast

Start your day with an early breakfast at your accommodation.

Drive to Gulmarg

Enjoy a scenic 2-hour drive to Gulmarg, known as the 'Meadow of Flowers,' situated at an altitude of 8,700 ft in the Pir Panjal range.

Gondola Ride

Take the world's second-highest gondola ride to Apharwat Peak (Phase 1 & 2) for stunning views of snow-capped Himalayan peaks (tickets not included in package).

Adventure Activities

Enjoy optional activities like skiing (in winter), golf at the world's highest golf course, or horseback riding through meadows (additional charges may apply).

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Sonmarg Excursion

Early Breakfast

Enjoy an early breakfast before your day trip.

Drive to Sonmarg

Embark on a picturesque 3-hour drive to Sonmarg, the 'Meadow of Gold,' located at an altitude of 9,200 ft along the Srinagar-Leh highway.

Thajiwas Glacier

Visit the magnificent Thajiwas Glacier, either by a short trek or pony ride (optional, fee not included).

Scenic Spots

Enjoy the pristine beauty of alpine meadows, gushing streams, and snow-capped mountains that make Sonmarg a photographer's paradise.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5: Pahalgam Excursion

Breakfast

Start your day with breakfast at your accommodation.

Drive to Pahalgam

Embark on a 3-hour drive to Pahalgam, the 'Valley of Shepherds,' passing through saffron fields and the beautiful Lidder Valley.

Local Sightseeing

Explore picturesque locations like Betaab Valley, Chandanwari, and Aru Valley by local taxi or pony ride (optional, fee not included).

Lidder River

Spend time by the gushing Lidder River and enjoy the pristine beauty of this mountain retreat.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 6: Departure

Breakfast

Enjoy your final breakfast at your accommodation.

Last-Minute Shopping

Time permitting, visit local markets for last-minute souvenir shopping for Kashmiri handicrafts and dry fruits.

Check-out

Complete check-out formalities from your accommodation.

Airport Transfer

Transfer to Srinagar International Airport for your onward journey with cherished memories of your Kashmir adventure.
