Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival Day – Srinagar
Arrive at Srinagar Airport
Meet our representative and transfer to the hotel. Visit Mughal Gardens — Chashma Shahi, Shalimar Bagh, and Nishat Bagh — and Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic city views. Visit a local handicrafts showroom for Kashmiri silk carpets and shawls. Enjoy a 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake. In the evening, stroll along Boulevard Road. Dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar hotel.
Day 2: Sonmarg Day Trip
Sonmarg (“Meadow of Gold”)
After breakfast, drive to Sonmarg (about 84 km from Srinagar). Visit Thajiwas Glacier (pony ride optional) or Zero Point. Enjoy scenic views of alpine meadows and snow-capped peaks. Return to Srinagar hotel for dinner and overnight stay.
Day 3: Gulmarg Day Trip
Gulmarg
After breakfast, drive to Gulmarg (51 km, 1–1.5 hours). Enjoy the scenic drive through Tangmarg. Optional Gondola ride to Kongdori or trek to Khilanmarg. Visit the world’s highest golf course. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.
Day 4: Srinagar – Pahalgam
Pahalgam (“Valley of Shepherds”)
After breakfast, transfer to Pahalgam. Enjoy the natural beauty, optional pony rides to Chandanwadi, or a walk along the Lidder River. Overnight stay in Pahalgam hotel.
Day 5: Pahalgam – Srinagar Houseboat
Pahalgam Sightseeing & Houseboat
After breakfast, enjoy Pahalgam sightseeing including Baisaran Valley (pony ride optional). Later, return to Srinagar and check into the houseboat. Dinner and overnight stay in houseboat.
Day 6: Departure
Departure
After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey.
