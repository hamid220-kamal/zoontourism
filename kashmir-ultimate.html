The Ultimate Kashmir Experience

Our 7-day Ultimate Kashmir Tour offers the most comprehensive journey through the paradise on earth. Experience the breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and authentic experiences across Srinagar, Gulmarg, Sonmarg, Pahalgam, and the hidden gem of Yusmarg.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

VIP Airport Welcome

Personalized welcome at Srinagar International Airport with traditional Kashmiri greetings by our representative who will assist with your transfer to the luxury hotel or premium houseboat.

Luxury Check-in

Settle into your premium accommodation with welcome drinks and traditional Kashmiri snacks.

Private Sunset Shikara Ride

Experience an exclusive evening Shikara ride on the pristine Dal Lake, gliding through floating gardens and witnessing the local lifestyle on the lake while enjoying tea and local snacks.

Special Welcome Dinner

Enjoy an authentic multi-course Wazwan dinner featuring traditional Kashmiri delicacies at your accommodation.

Day 2: Srinagar Heritage Tour

Gourmet Breakfast

Begin your day with a lavish Kashmiri breakfast at your accommodation.

Mughal Gardens Tour

Visit the spectacular Mughal Gardens including Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), Shalimar Bagh (Garden of Love), and Chashme Shahi (Royal Spring), featuring terraced lawns, cascading fountains, and exquisite flowerbeds with expert guide insights into their historical significance.

Historical Sites with Expert Guide

Visit the sacred Hazratbal Shrine with its white marble elegance, the ancient wooden Khanqah of Shah Hamadan showcasing traditional Kashmiri architecture, and the hilltop Shankaracharya Temple offering panoramic views of the city.

Artisan Workshops

Exclusive visits to master craftsmen workshops to witness the creation of Kashmiri handicrafts including pashmina weaving, carpet making, and papier-mâché artistry with opportunities to purchase directly from artisans.

Return to Accommodation

Return to your accommodation for a gourmet dinner featuring fusion Kashmiri cuisine and overnight stay.

Day 3: Gulmarg Adventure

Early Breakfast

Start your day with an energizing breakfast at your accommodation.

Scenic Drive to Gulmarg

Embark on a picturesque 2-hour drive to Gulmarg, the 'Meadow of Flowers', situated at an altitude of 8,700 ft. Stop at scenic viewpoints along the way for photo opportunities.

Premium Gondola Experience

Enjoy priority access to the world's second-highest gondola ride to Apharwat Peak (Phase 1 & 2), offering breathtaking views of the surrounding Himalayan ranges (tickets included).

Premium Activities

Choose from premium activities including golf at the world's highest golf course, professional guided skiing (in winter), or a guided nature walk with picnic lunch in the meadows.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for a relaxing evening with dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Sonmarg Expedition

Breakfast

Enjoy a hearty breakfast before your day trip.

Drive to Sonmarg

Embark on a scenic 3-hour drive to Sonmarg, the 'Meadow of Gold', located at 9,200 ft along the ancient Silk Route. Travel through pine forests and villages with stops at panoramic viewpoints.

Thajiwas Glacier Experience

Visit the magnificent Thajiwas Glacier with options for guided trekking or horseback riding through snow-covered landscapes (included in package).

Mountain Picnic Lunch

Enjoy a specially prepared mountain picnic lunch with hot beverages in a scenic location.

Riverside Relaxation

Spend time beside the Sindh River, soaking in the pristine mountain views and fresh air.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5: Pahalgam Valley

Breakfast

Start your day with a nutritious breakfast at your accommodation.

Scenic Drive to Pahalgam

Embark on a scenic 3-hour drive to Pahalgam, the 'Valley of Shepherds', passing through saffron fields, traditional villages, and the breathtaking Lidder Valley with stops at an authentic saffron farm.

Valley Exploration

Private guided tours of Betaab Valley, Chandanwari, and Aru Valley in a dedicated local vehicle, accessing spots off the typical tourist routes.

Lidder River Activities

Optional activities by the Lidder River including guided fishing excursion or riverside meditation session.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 6: Yusmarg - Hidden Gem

Breakfast

Enjoy breakfast at your accommodation.

Drive to Yusmarg

Embark on a 2-hour drive to the less-explored Yusmarg, a pristine meadow surrounded by pine forests and snow-capped mountains.

Nature Walk

Guided nature walk through pine forests to Doodh Ganga River, known for its crystal clear waters.

Meadow Exploration

Explore the beautiful meadows and surrounding hills with optional horseback riding through this untouched paradise.

Authentic Experience

Visit a local Gujjar family home for traditional Kashmiri noon chai (salt tea) and local snacks for an authentic cultural experience.

Return to Srinagar

Return to your accommodation in Srinagar for a special farewell dinner featuring live music and overnight stay.

Day 7: Departure

Breakfast

Enjoy your final breakfast at your accommodation.

Optional Early Morning Lake View

For early risers, enjoy a final peaceful morning view of Dal Lake with complimentary coffee service.

Curated Shopping Experience

Visit select boutique shops for last-minute authentic Kashmiri souvenirs with expert guidance to ensure quality purchases.

Check-out

Complete check-out formalities with assistance from our staff.

VIP Airport Transfer

Comfortable transfer to Srinagar International Airport for your onward journey with a farewell gift hamper of Kashmiri specialties.
